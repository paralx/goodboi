About Us

Good Boi News was started with the intention of bringing a smile to people's faces with wholesome news about animals from all over the world.

Our team scours the internet for stories of heroic pets, heartwarming rescues, and other uplifting animal news that will brighten your day.

We hope that our website can be a source of positivity and joy in a world that often seems to be filled with negativity and sadness.
